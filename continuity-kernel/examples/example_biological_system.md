# Example: Forest Ecosystem

## Subject

Temperate Forest Ecosystem

## Continuity Analysis

### Subject Continuity

Long-term persistence of ecological relationships across generations.

### Inherited Structures

* Nutrient cycles
* Species relationships
* Soil networks
* Reproductive pathways

### Integrity Strengths

* Diversity
* Redundancy
* Distributed stewardship

### Continuity Debts

* Invasive species
* Habitat fragmentation
* Nutrient depletion

### Failure Modes

* Habitat Loss
* Ecological Cascade
* Predation Imbalance
* Continuity Drift

### Adaptive Failures

Small fires may appear destructive locally while preserving continuity globally.

### Thesis

Healthy ecosystems maintain continuity through distributed inheritance rather than centralized control.
