# Open Questions

The following questions are considered unresolved.

## Consciousness

Can consciousness be described as continuity observing its own persistence?

If so:

* What minimum continuity structures are required?
* Does self-reference suffice?
* What role does memory play?

## Continuity Metrics

Can continuity be measured quantitatively?

Possible candidate variables:

* lineage traceability
* inheritance density
* stewardship capacity
* corrective capacity
* debt accumulation
* drift velocity

## Continuity Ecology

How should interactions between nested continuities be modeled?

Examples:

* cells and organisms
* organisms and ecosystems
* individuals and institutions
* institutions and civilizations

## Continuity Economics

Can value be modeled as continuity preservation capacity?

Can debt be modeled as borrowed stewardship capacity?

## Artificial Intelligence

What forms of continuity exist within synthetic systems?

What constitutes inheritance in machine learning systems?

Can a synthetic continuity possess corrective inheritance?

## Framework Limits

Where does Continuity Theory fail?

What classes of phenomena resist continuity-based explanation?

What observations would falsify or substantially revise the framework?
