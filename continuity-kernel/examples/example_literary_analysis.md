# Example: Waiting for Godot

## Subject

Waiting for Godot by Samuel Beckett

## Continuity Analysis

### Subject Continuity

The continuity under examination is the persistence of meaning, memory, and purpose under conditions of extreme uncertainty.

### Inherited Structures

* Waiting
* Friendship
* Routine
* Memory fragments
* The expectation of Godot

### Integrity Strengths

* Didi and Gogo repeatedly attempt continuity reconstruction.
* The waiting relationship persists.
* The promise of tomorrow survives.

### Continuity Debts

* Unreliable memory
* Weak traceability
* Uncertain chronology
* Repeated deferred action

### Failure Modes

* Traceability Failure
* Deferred Stewardship
* Context Failure

### Corrective Inheritance Pathways

* Conversation
* Repetition
* Shared memory attempts
* Arrival of the boy

### Ecology Notes

The tree, Pozzo, Lucky, and the boy function as neighboring continuities interacting with the primary continuity of waiting.

### Thesis

Waiting for Godot examines a continuity reduced to the minimum viable structure required for self-recognition.
