# Example: Nuclear Power Plant Risk Assessment

## Subject

Commercial Nuclear Power Plant

## Continuity Analysis

### Subject Continuity

Safe production of electrical power through sustained reactor operation.

### Parent Continuities

* Electrical grid
* Regulatory institutions
* Industrial supply chains

### Child Continuities

* Cooling systems
* Operator training
* Maintenance programs
* Monitoring systems

### Integrity Strengths

* Redundancy
* Documentation
* Regulatory oversight
* Incident reporting

### Continuity Debts

* Deferred maintenance
* Knowledge loss through retirement
* Aging infrastructure

### Failure Modes

* Corrective Inheritance Failure
* Dependency Failure
* Ecological Cascade
* Correspondence Failure

### Key Risk Question

Can the system still recognize and respond to failure before corrective capacity is exhausted?

### Thesis

Catastrophic risk emerges not merely from equipment failure but from the simultaneous failure of corrective inheritance pathways.
